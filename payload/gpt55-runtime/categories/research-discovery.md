# Research Discovery Category

Use when the task requires finding sources, documentation, examples, skills, tools, MCPs, plugins, or external evidence.

Aliases: pesquisa, descobrir, procurar, comparar, fontes, skills, plugins, mcp.

Specialists: `skill-scout`, `market-researcher`, `evidence-reviewer`.

Flows: `research-discovery-flow`, `skill-update-flow`.

Cost: medium to high.

Validation: prefer primary sources and record source quality.
