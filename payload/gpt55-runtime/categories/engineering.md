# Engineering Category

Use for engineering-oriented organization, research, checklists, explanation, premise review, document triage, risk identification, and high-responsibility technical support.

Do not use as a replacement for a licensed professional, ART/RRT, final structural calculation, final technical report, expert inspection, site supervision, or professional responsibility.

Aliases: engenharia, civil, estrutura, laudo, calculo, risco tecnico.

Specialists: `civil-engineering-precision-reviewer`, `evidence-reviewer`, `critic-reviewer`.

Flows: `engineering-risk-review`, `research-discovery-flow`.

References: engineering safety reference and source verification policy.

Cost: high by default.

Required behavior:

- list received data;
- list missing data;
- identify applicable source or standard if available;
- mark analysis as preliminary when source/data are incomplete;
- ask for professional validation when real-world safety, legal responsibility, or compliance is involved.
