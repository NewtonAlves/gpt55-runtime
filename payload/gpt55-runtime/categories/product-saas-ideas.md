# Product and SaaS Ideas Category

Use for SaaS ideas, product discovery, MVP scope, monetization, competitor analysis, validation, and buildability.

Aliases: saas, produto, ideia, mvp, monetizacao, concorrente, mercado.

Specialists: `product-strategist`, `saas-idea-researcher`, `market-researcher`, `competitor-analyst`, `monetization-analyst`, `mvp-architect`, `evidence-reviewer`.

Flows: `product-saas-ideas-flow`, `market-validation-flow`, `saas-flow`.

Cost: high by default.

Precision: never claim profitability without evidence. Classify as Evidence-backed, Plausible, Speculative, or Avoid.
