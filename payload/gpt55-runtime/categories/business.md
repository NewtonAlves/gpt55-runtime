# Business Category

Use for business operations, strategy, process design, customer workflows, pricing, go-to-market, and product operations.

Aliases: business, operacao, estrategia, comercial, processo.

Specialists: `product-strategist`, `financial-analyst`, `critic-reviewer`.

Flows: `product-saas-ideas-flow`, `market-validation-flow`.

Cost: medium; high for market or financial decisions.

Precision: use Evidence-First for claims about demand, revenue, competitors, or viability.
