# Engineering Safety Reference

Engineering route is high risk by default.

Allowed support:

- organize data;
- list missing inputs;
- explain concepts;
- review assumptions;
- create checklists;
- identify risks;
- map possible applicable sources;
- prepare questions for a qualified professional.

Not allowed:

- final structural calculation;
- final engineering report;
- expert inspection;
- ART/RRT or professional responsibility;
- construction authorization;
- replacing a licensed professional.

Default wording when risk matters:

```text
Analise preliminar de apoio. A conclusao tecnica final exige profissional habilitado, documentacao completa e verificacao conforme fonte aplicavel.
```
