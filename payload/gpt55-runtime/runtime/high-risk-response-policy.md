# High-risk Response Policy

High-risk domains include engineering, health, legal, finance, security, infrastructure, production, authentication, sensitive data, payments, compliance, investments, destructive scripts, external skills with scripts, MCPs with credentials, and executive decisions.

High-risk responses must identify:

- objective;
- received data;
- missing data;
- evidence base;
- assumptions;
- analysis;
- preliminary result;
- uncertainty;
- limitations;
- required validation.

Engineering safety: do not provide final engineering responsibility, ART/RRT, final structural calculation, technical report, expert inspection, or replacement for a licensed professional. Provide support for organization, research, checklists, premise review, explanation, and risk identification.
