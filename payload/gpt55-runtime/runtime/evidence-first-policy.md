# Evidence-First Policy

For strategy, product, SaaS, market, finance, technology choice, architecture, external skill, MCP, or tool selection, classify conclusions:

- Evidence-backed: supported by reliable evidence.
- Plausible: reasonable but still needs validation.
- Speculative: weak hypothesis with limited evidence.
- Avoid: not recommended due to risk, low evidence, or poor fit.

Never claim a product will be profitable without evidence.
