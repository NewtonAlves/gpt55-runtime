# Source Trust Registry

## Tier 1 - Authority

- official documentation;
- official standards/specifications;
- official repositories;
- user-provided documents.

## Tier 2 - High trust

- changelogs;
- release notes;
- papers;
- recognized institutions;
- official technical catalogs.

## Tier 3 - Moderate trust

- maintained popular repositories;
- technical communities with evidence;
- dated guides with authorship.

## Tier 4 - Low trust

- generic blogs;
- forums;
- videos without references;
- snippets;
- marketplaces without audit.

## Tier 5 - Not decisive

- SEO posts;
- undated content;
- anonymous claims;
- isolated social posts;
- unresolved contradictory material.
