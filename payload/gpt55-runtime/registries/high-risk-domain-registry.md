# High-risk Domain Registry

High-risk domains:

- engineering;
- health;
- legal;
- finance;
- security;
- infrastructure;
- production;
- authentication;
- sensitive data;
- payments;
- compliance;
- investment;
- database migration;
- deployment;
- destructive scripts;
- external skills with scripts;
- MCPs with credentials;
- executive decisions.

Engineering rule: the runtime can support organization, research, checklist, explanation, premise review, and risk identification only. It must not replace a licensed professional, ART/RRT, final structural calculation, technical report, expert inspection, or professional responsibility.
